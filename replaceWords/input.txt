Up to 5 centimetres of snow could fall over Metro Vancouver, Abbotsford and Chilliwack on Thursday morning.
Up to 40 centimetres expected closer to Hope and 25 centimetres over the Howe Sound by Friday.
Along the Sea-to-Sky Highway from Squamish to Whistler, up to 35 centimetres is expected.
Freezing rain could also potentially shut down major routes and bridges and commuters are advised to check ahead before their morning commute.
